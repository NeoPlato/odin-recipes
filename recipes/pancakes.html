Buttermilk Oatmeal Pancakes

Description

Delicious pancakes that are a great way to start the day!

Prep: 10 mins

Cook: 15 mins

Total: 25 mins

Servings: 4

Yield: 4 servings

Ingredients

  • 2 cups buttermilk
  • 1 ½ cups rulled oats
  • ½ cup all-purpose flour
  • ½ cup cornmeal
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 ½ teaspoons white sugar
  • ½ teaspoon baking soda
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • ¼ cup vegetable oil

Steps

  1. Mix buttermilk and oats in a large bowl; allow to rest about 5 minutes
  2. Stir flour, cornmeal, baking powder, sugar, baking soda, and salt into oat mixture.
  3. Add eggs and vegetable oil; mix until just combined.
  4. Heat a lightly oiled griddle over medium-high heat.
  5. Drop batter by large spoonfuls onto the griddle; cook until bubbles form and the edges are dry, about 3 minutes.
  6. Flip and cook until browned on the other side, about 3 minutes more.
  7. Repeat with remaining batter.
